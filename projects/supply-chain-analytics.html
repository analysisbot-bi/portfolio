Supply Chain Performance Analytics & Delay-Risk Prediction

Small web app + Power BI for shipment performance. Scores delay risk so ops can react before a late delivery only shows up in a monthly report.

XGBoostSHAPPower BI PythonStreamlit

The problem

Delays often show up too late. Once they're in a monthly report you've already got missed deliveries and stuck stock. I wanted something that flags risk earlier and doesn't need a data science background to open.

What I did

Pipeline from raw trade/customs data to a risk score and a KPI view:

  • Preprocessing: log-transforms on skewed value/mass fields.
  • Features: HS chapter, origin/destination encodings, route flags, usage flags.
  • Model: tuned XGBoost on ~44k held-out shipments.
  • SHAP: beeswarm + waterfall so a prediction can be explained.
  • Dashboard: eligibility mix, high-risk origins/commodities, risk-score spread.

Results

XGBoost landed at 86.9% accuracy and ROC-AUC 0.96. SHAP pointed at usage flags, origin, route, and HS chapter. Roughly 36% of shipments sat in the not-eligible band.

Notes

The model score mattered, but the dashboard mattered more. If ops can't read it, it's just a notebook.
