Supply Chain Performance Analytics & Delay-Risk Prediction

A web-based decision-support system wired to Power BI that watches supply-chain performance in real time and flags delay risk before it becomes a disruption — moving teams from firefighting to foresight.

Power BIPredictive AnalyticsDashboards Delay RiskDecision SupportForecasting Business IntelligenceWeb App

The problem

Supply chains fail quietly. By the time a delay is visible in a monthly report, the damage — missed deliveries, idle inventory, unhappy customers — is already done. The challenge was to give teams real-time visibility and an early warning for delays, in a format anyone could use without a data-science background.

My approach

I designed an end-to-end system that combines predictive analytics with an accessible web-based interface and live Power BI dashboards:

  • Real-time monitoring of key supply-chain performance indicators.
  • Predictive analytics to identify potential delays before they happen.
  • Interactive Power BI dashboards that make the numbers legible at a glance.
  • A web platform so insights are shareable and teams can collaborate across functions.

The impact

The system was built to drive concrete outcomes: improved operational efficiency, fewer delays and disruptions, and better visibility across the entire chain. Most of all, it enables smarter, faster decisions — shifting an organisation from reactive problem-solving to proactive strategy.

What I took away

This project sits right where my two worlds meet — data science and business operations. Building it reinforced that the best analytics work isn't the most complex model; it's the one that lands in front of a decision-maker at the right moment, in a form they instantly trust.
