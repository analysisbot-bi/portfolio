01. About Me

I'm Vickram — a data person at heart. What started as curiosity about why the numbers behave the way they do turned into an MSc in Computer Science at Coventry University, where I now live at the intersection of Big Data analytics, machine learning, and clear, honest storytelling with data.

I earned my B.Tech at SRM Institute of Science & Technology in Chennai, then packed my bags for the UK to go deeper. These days I'm most at home writing Python, wrangling messy datasets through cleaning and feature engineering, training and tuning neural networks (ANN) and ensemble models like Random Forest, and then turning the results into Power BI dashboards and forecasts that a non-technical stakeholder can actually act on.

Here's the thing that makes me a little different: before I fell for data, I ran business operations and led finance teams. So I don't just chase accuracy scores — I care about the decision on the other side of the model. I like building things that move a real metric, not just a confusion matrix.
