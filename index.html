Who I Am · Data Scientist in the Making

01. About Me

I'm Vickram — a data person at heart. What started as curiosity about why the numbers behave the way they do turned into an MSc in Computer Science at Coventry University, where I now live at the intersection of Big Data analytics, machine learning, and clear, honest storytelling with data.

I earned my B.Tech at SRM Institute of Science & Technology in Chennai, then moved to the UK to go deeper. Day to day I'm most at home writing Python and SQL, wrangling messy datasets through cleaning and feature engineering, and building machine-learning models — from neural networks (ANN) and Random Forest to NLP text classification and SLA-breach prediction. I then turn the results into Power BI dashboards and reports a non-technical stakeholder can actually act on.

A lot of my recent work centres on customer-support analytics — end-to-end pipelines, predictive models and BI dashboards — and you can explore all of the code on my GitHub.

Here's the thing that makes me a little different: before I fell for data, I ran business operations and led finance teams. So I don't just chase accuracy scores — I care about the decision on the other side of the model. I like building things that move a real metric, not just a confusion matrix.
