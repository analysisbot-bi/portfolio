01. About Me

I'm Vickram — a Data Analyst in the making. What started as curiosity about why the numbers behave the way they do turned into an MSc in Computer Science at Coventry University, where I focus on data analysis, machine learning, and clear storytelling with charts and dashboards.

I earned my B.Tech at SRM Institute of Science & Technology in Chennai, then moved to the UK to go deeper. Day to day I'm most at home writing Python and SQL, wrangling messy datasets, building machine-learning models, and turning results into Power BI dashboards and clear data visualisations a stakeholder can act on.

Two projects I highlight here are my supply-chain delay-risk DSS and my obesity-level ANN classifier — and you can explore further work on my GitHub.

Here's the thing that makes me a little different: before I fell for data, I ran business operations and led finance teams. So I don't just chase accuracy scores — I care about the decision on the other side of the model. I like building things that move a real metric, not just a confusion matrix.
