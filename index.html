About

I'm Vickram, an aspiring Data Analyst. I'm doing an MSc in Computer Science at Coventry University, focused on data analysis, machine learning, and dashboards.

I did my B.Tech at SRM Institute of Science & Technology in Chennai, then moved to the UK. Day to day I write Python and SQL, clean datasets, train models, and put results into Power BI and other data visualisations.
